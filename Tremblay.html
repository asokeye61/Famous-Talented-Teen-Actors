Jacob Tremblay

Born: October 5, 2006
Born in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada
Occupations: Actor

Biography

Jacob Tremblay (/ˈtrɒmbleɪ/ TROM-blay; born October 5, 2006) is a Canadian actor. He became known for his role as a child born in captivity in Room (2015), for which he won the Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Young Performer and became the youngest nominee for the Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Supporting Role. Tremblay subsequently starred as a child with Treacher Collins syndrome in the drama Wonder (2017) and a naive sixth grader in the comedy Good Boys (2019). He has since taken on voice roles as Damian Wayne / Robin in Harley Quinn (2019), the title characters in Luca (2021) and Orion and the Dark (2024), which earned him an Emmy Win, and Flounder in The Little Mermaid (2023).

Notable Contributions:

  • Has Won a crtics choice award for his critically acclaimed Movie Room And won an emmy for orion and the dark
  • His debut Movie was Smurfs 2
  • Was nominated for the 2015 SAG Awards Best Male Actor in a Supporting Role, becoming the youngest actor nominated for that award.
